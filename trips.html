"Kakhisi lake 2022"

Kakhi Lake is located in Borjomi region, 1752 meters above sea level. There are forests around the lake, thanks to which the air is fresh and clean, although it is cold due to the altitude. Lake Kakhisi, due to its beauty, is one of the most popular hiking spots in Georgia, which can be reached both by car and on foot, there is no short trail. Due to the bad road, it is very difficult to travel by car, so only an off-road vehicle can reach the lake, which is an additional pleasure for off-road enthusiasts.
Lake Kakhi can appear as "a place cut off from civilization" because it does not catch the phone and therefore the Internet. It's a great opportunity to put technology and lifestyle aside for a while, enjoy the beautiful views and spend some time surrounded by people. In the forest, they are animals, although a little dog running by a low tent might scare you.
